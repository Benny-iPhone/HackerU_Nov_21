
Create an app that plays radio station(s)

1. Button for play/pause
2. Slider for volume
3. Segmented control for selecting stations

4. here you can find streaming urls for radio stations:
http://www.radiosure.com/stations/

5. you will need to enable http networking in your info.plist file (google it) 
6. AVPlayer is the class for playing audio from streaming url
7. Bonus: run on a real device, and make it playable when the app in background
